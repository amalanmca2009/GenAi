Common Editing & Navigation Shortcuts
Ctrl+C: Copy
Ctrl+X: Cut (or cut entire line without selection)
Ctrl+V: Paste
Ctrl+Z: Undo
Ctrl+Y: Redo
Ctrl+F: Find
Ctrl+H: Replace
F12: Go to definition
Shift+F12: Find all references
Ctrl+K, Ctrl+C: Comment selection
Ctrl+K, Ctrl+U: Uncomment selection
Ctrl+.: Quick Actions and Refactorings
